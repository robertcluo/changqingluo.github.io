Brief Biography

Changqing Luo is currently a Ph. D. student, advised by Dr. Pan Li in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at Case Western Reserve University. He received his B.E. and M.E. degree in Telecommunication Engineering and Communication and Information Systems from Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications, Chongqing, China, in 2004 and 2007, respectively, and his previous Ph. D. degree in Communication and Information Systems from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, Beijing, China, in 2011. He was a Lecturer in the School of Computer Science and Technology at Huazhong University of Science and Technology, China.
